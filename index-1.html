02 — Case Studies
CIAZ Photography Wedding Quote Builder
Client Project

Coordinating a multi-round product build for a live business

A wedding photography business needed a web-based quote builder — a multi-step tool with live pricing and WhatsApp integration so clients could get instant estimates. I worked directly with the business owner to gather requirements, test each build, and feed back what needed to change.

Across several iteration rounds I coordinated fixes for calculation errors, a luxury UI/UX redesign, an iOS Safari compatibility issue, and a reordering of the step flow based on how real clients were using it — translating business feedback into clear, actionable change requests each time.

My role was requirements gathering, testing, and iteration coordination — not the underlying code. In interviews I speak to the process: how I structured feedback, prioritized fixes, and kept the build aligned with what the client actually needed.

During my internship, I was placed on a full-stack web development team and got close exposure to how a real project moves from idea to build — frontend, backend, and database pieces all fitting together, and how a development team plans and sequences that work.
